Take Care of your Clothes


General Care

Immmidiately as you get home try wash the clothes hand wash or machine wash. Treat the items with gentle care. Air drying is your good friend as it helps mintain an extend the life of the item. You're not just keeping them in good shapee , you're extending their journey with intention.

Knit

Hand wash cold and dry flat. Avoid hanging. The natural fibres of the wool will change the garment over time resulting in a brushed, texture finish. To restore the garment to its original state, use a pilling comb or fabric shaver

Denim

Cold hand wash separately, wash inside out. Colour may transfer when new. We recommend hanging or folding the piece when storing and either ironing or steaming post wash. Use a soft detergent, avoid over-washing to extend the lifespan of your denim piece.

Cotton Items

Cold wash with mild detergent, and air dry or tumble dry on low heat. Avoid over-drying, and iron to remove wrinkles. Proper storage in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight, can also help extend the life of your cotton items.

Synthetic Fibers

Cold wash with mild detergent, and air dry or tumble dry on low heat. Avoid over-drying, and iron to remove wrinkles.

Silk

Cold wash with mild detergent. Hang to dry or tumble dry on low heat. Iron whilst still damp to remove wrinkles.

Delicate Fabrics

This includes lace and velvet.Cold wash with mild detergent. Hang to dry or tumble dry on low heat. Use a cool iron or steam iron with a pressing cloth to prevent damage.
